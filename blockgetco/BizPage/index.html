    About Us

    Blockget formed from a hackathon event London sponsored by Decent.

    Our Mission

    To lower costs and make storage more accessible globally. No need for bank accounts. Anyone can store documents securely. We propose later solutions to allow biometrics via zero-knowledge proofs.

    Our Plan

    To use the Decent SDK and APIs to implement a management layer for storage. This layer connects storage services and the Decent blockchain.

    Our Vision

    Anyone at any location can store images, documents, biometric details (securely) all without the worry of where the storage would actually take place - all from a phone.
